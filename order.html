How To Order Custom Goods:

Don't be shy! Contact Us

Let us know what you are intrested in. We've made all kinds of 'things' and we're always looking for a challenge.

Whether its a one of a kind hanger, unique hooks for crafty coat racks, hand crafted garden tools, or personalized gifts,

if it's custom and it's steel we can do it! All of our products are made to order so expect a lead time anywhere from 2-6

weeks depending on the item. Our head blacksmith is also an ADDA certified drafter in case you need a little help with design.

Large projects require a 1/3 non-refundable deposit for materials, and are to be paid in full prior to delivery.
